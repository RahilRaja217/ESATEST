# ESA New to Embedded Services (Compact Onboarding)

Welcome to Sage Embedded Services (ESA). This quick start guide helps you understand where to begin learning and what the platform offers.

## For first-time learners

ESA is a platform that helps partners embed finance capabilities (accounting, expense, payroll, carbon) directly into their applications, so end users experience finance workflows in-context.

## Where to start

| Learning path | What you'll understand | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Architecture | How ESA is designed and how pieces fit together | Technical leads, architects, integration teams |
| APIs | What capabilities are exposed and how to call them | Developers, technical delivery |
| Availability | What is ready now and what's coming | Product managers, commercial teams, pre-sales |
| Marketing | The value proposition and competitive positioning | Sales teams, executives, partners |
| Tenders | How to answer RFI/RFP questions with evidence | Pre-sales, bid teams |

## Typical first steps

1. **Understand the basics**: Start with architecture to see the big picture.
2. **Explore capabilities**: Ask about APIs or availability to see what's live today.
3. **Prepare for your role**: Ask for role-specific content (marketing, architecture, tenders).
4. **Drill into detail**: Pick a capability and ask implementation questions.
