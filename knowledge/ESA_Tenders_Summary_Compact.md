# ESA Tenders Summary (Compact)

Sage Embedded Services (ESA) can support partners and the pre-sales team in responding to RFI and RFP tender questionnaires by providing grounded, consistent answers about capabilities, availability, integration approach, and competitive positioning.

## Core tender support value

ESA helps partners answer tender questions confidently by combining documented capability evidence, availability data, proposition content, and real-world reference examples.

## Tender support areas

| Tender area | What ESA can help answer | Typical tender question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Capability coverage | Which finance capabilities are available and in which regions | What accounting or payroll capabilities are supported? |
| Availability and roadmap | What is live, externally marketable, or internal by region | What is currently live in the UK or US? |
| Integration and architecture | How ESA integrates with partner platforms and systems | How does ESA connect with existing partner workflows? |
| Security and compliance | Platform security controls, data governance, and certifications | What security standards does ESA comply with? |
| Commercial and proposition | Pricing model, partner engagement, and value delivery | How is ESA commercially structured for partners? |
| Reference examples | Existing partner use cases that demonstrate real-world delivery | Can ESA provide examples of live partner implementations? |

## When supporting tender responses

- Ground every answer in documented evidence; avoid unsupported claims.
- Use availability status labels exactly: Live, Externally Marketable, Internal.
- Link capability breadth to the partner's specific use case and buyer outcomes.
- For sensitive or confidential tender content, confirm what can be shared externally.
