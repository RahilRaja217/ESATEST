# ESA Marketing Proposition Summary (Compact)

Sage Embedded Services (ESA) is positioned as a way for partners to embed trusted finance capabilities directly into their own customer journeys, reducing friction between business workflows and financial operations.

## Core proposition

ESA helps partners deliver finance outcomes inside their product experience by combining embedded APIs, configurable integration patterns, and domain capabilities.

## Value pillars

| Value pillar | What it means in practice | Typical buyer outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Faster time to market | Reuse embedded finance capabilities rather than building from scratch | Launch differentiated finance features sooner |
| Lower delivery and change cost | Standardized APIs and modular capabilities reduce custom build effort | Improve delivery efficiency and maintainability |
| Better end-user experience | Finance workflows appear in-context within partner journeys | Increase adoption and reduce process handoffs |
| Trusted scale and governance | Platform controls, security, and operational patterns support growth | Improve confidence in reliability and compliance |

## Positioning themes

- Embedded by design: ESA is designed for integration into partner products and channels.
- Capability-led: Partners can adopt accounting, expense, carbon, and payroll capabilities progressively.
- Practical modernization: ESA supports transformation without requiring full platform rebuilds.

## When explaining ESA marketing value

- Start with customer outcomes before technical detail.
- Link each capability to a business problem and measurable benefit.
- Keep language simple, practical, and confidence-building.
