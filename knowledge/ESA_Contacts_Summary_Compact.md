# ESA Contacts Summary (Compact)

## Purpose
This artifact supports fast, consistent answers for users who want to identify the right contact within or adjacent to Embedded Services. It is the sole authoritative source for contact lookups in the ESA agent.

## Column definitions
- **Name**: Full name of the contact.
- **Registered Role**: The contact's official job title.
- **Department**: The team or function the contact belongs to.
- **Reports to**: The contact's direct line manager.
- **AKA Role**: The contextual role or label used when identifying this contact for ESA purposes.
- **ES**: Whether the contact is employed within Embedded Services. Yes = Embedded Services employee. No = Adjacent or partner team.

## Recommended answer structure for contact questions
1. Identify the best primary contact by role, department, or AKA role.
2. State their name, registered role, and ES status.
3. Include reporting line if it adds useful context.
4. If multiple contacts match, list the primary first, then alternatives.
5. If the match is ambiguous, ask one narrowing follow-up question.
